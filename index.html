ABOUT MY WORK

From flawless complexion products to iconic ambient lighting powders, Hourglass combines science and artistry to create products that enhance natural beauty while delivering high-performance results. Now part of the Unilever Prestige division, Hourglass is available in thousands of retail locations worldwide and continues to grow as a leader in luxury cosmetics.
